Key Features

Malty stands out from its competitors by beating them on cost, usability, and depth.

Cost-effective

Rather than shelling out for outside retail data agencies, Malty works with businesses to create a solution in-house. By getting more out of each business, Malty is better able to leverage existing connections and dig deep.

Superior Usability

Malty's dashboard shows you events of interest, like spikes and drops in demand, as well as longer-term trends, letting you decide the level of granularity you want. With state-of-the-art data visualization, Malty turns massive amounts of data into actionable business insights.

Depth of Analysis

Malty enables you to ask hard questions of your data: where should we advertise? How do we increase high-margin sales? Who are we missing out on? It does this not by sitting people down in focus groups, but by examining their real-world behavior. Consider Malty your constant pilot.

Machine-Learning Enabled

Increase the resolution of your data by interpolating lower-granularity datasets - turning any dataset into an hour-by-hour inference. Leverage machine learning to increase the quality of ALL your data with Malty's additional insight.
